Academic Experience & Credentials

Providing scholarly editing with professional expertise. Learn about my academic background and consultant history.

Editorial Philosophy

I specialize in high-impact publication preparation.

My name is Dr. Claire Sinclair, and I am an independent academic editing consultant. Unlike large agencies that handle manuscripts as volume products, I review every manuscript individually. My goal is simple: to make your manuscript speak with clarity, precision, and academic authority.

I hold a PhD in English and Comparative Literature from Columbia University, with over 15 years of experience refereeing journals, editing manuscripts for academic presses, and advising faculty on grant applications.

  • Doctoral Credential: PhD from an Ivy League university with active journal review assignments.
  • Philosophy: I focus on logical structure and rhetorical flow, not just grammatical typos.
  • Direct Collaboration: You converse directly with me, ensuring subject specialism and secure data control.
