About the Editor

I specialize in high-impact publication preparation.

My name is Dr. Claire Sinclair, and I am an independent academic editing consultant. Unlike large agencies that handle manuscripts as volume products, I review every manuscript individually. My goal is simple: to make your manuscript speak with clarity, precision, and academic authority.

I work directly with scholars to refine their research into polished, publication-ready manuscripts, ensuring their voices are heard and their arguments are clearly articulated.

My Approach

Editing Philosophy

"I focus on logical structure and rhetorical flow, not just grammatical typos. True academic editing goes beyond surface corrections; it elevates the clarity and impact of your intellectual contribution."

  • Clarity Over Jargon: Simplifying complex ideas without losing academic rigor.
  • Structural Integrity: Ensuring a logical progression of arguments.
  • Direct Collaboration: You converse directly with me, ensuring subject specialism and secure data control.
